About Dr.Venu Madhav

Dr. Venu Madhav: Expert Orthopedic Surgeon with 18 Years of Experience

With over 18 years of dedicated experience in orthopedic care, Dr. Venu Madhav has become a trusted name in the field of musculoskeletal health. He is known for his exceptional expertise in treating a wide range of orthopedic conditions, including joint replacements, sports injuries, fractures, and spine disorders.

Dr. Venu Madhav’s commitment to excellence has earned him numerous accolades, including the Best Paper Presentation Award at APCON 2008, reflecting his active contribution to advancing orthopedic knowledge and practices. His passion for continuous learning and professional development is evident through his memberships in prominent organizations such as:

  • Indian Orthopedic Association
  • Shoulder and Elbow Society of India
  • Deccan Arthroscopy Society

These affiliations allows Dr. Venu Madhav to stay at the forefront of the latest advancements in orthopedic treatments, ensuring his patients receive the highest standard of care. Whether it's through innovative surgery techniques or personalized rehabilitation plans, Dr. Venu Madhav is dedicated to improving his patients' quality of life and helping them regain optimal mobility.
