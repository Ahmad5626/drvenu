A Minimally Invasive Solution for Joint Problems

Arthroscopy is a cutting-edge procedure that allows for the diagnosis and treatment of joint issues with minimal invasion. Dr. Venu Madhav, a top orthopedic doctor in Hyderabad, specializes in performing arthroscopic surgeries to help patients regain mobility and lead pain-free lives.

What is Arthroscopy?

Arthroscopy is a minimally invasive surgical technique where a small camera, called an arthroscope, is inserted into the joint through a tiny incision. This allows the doctor to visualize, diagnose, and treat joint problems without the need for large incisions.

Common Conditions Treated with Arthroscopy:
  • Knee Injuries: Meniscus tears, ligament injuries (e.g., ACL), cartilage damage.
  • Shoulder Problems: Rotator cuff tears, frozen shoulder, labral tears.
  • Hip, Elbow, and Wrist Conditions: Impingements, ligament repairs, and more.
Benefits of Arthroscopy:
  • Minimally Invasive: Smaller incisions mean less pain and quicker recovery.
  • Precise Diagnosis: High-definition visualization ensures accurate treatment.
  • Faster Recovery: Return to daily activities sooner than with traditional surgery.
