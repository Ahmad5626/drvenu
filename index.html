FAQs

Common symptoms that may indicate a need for an orthopedic consultation include persistent joint pain, swelling or stiffness in joints, difficulty in movement, sudden injuries like fractures or sprains, back or neck pain, and numbness or tingling in limbs. If these symptoms affect daily activities, consulting an orthopedician is advised.

During your first visit, Dr Venu will review your medical history, discuss your symptoms, and conduct a physical examination. They may order imaging tests, such as X-rays or MRIs, to gain a clearer understanding of your condition. Based on the findings, a personalized treatment plan will be discussed.
